Frequently Asked Questions

1. How many days after the payment will I receive the essays?

AFTER you complete the survey and provide all the information we need to write your essays, it takes 5 working days to finish Personal Statement. It takes 3 working days to finish Resume. It takes 6 working days to finish Recommendation letters (2 or 3).

2. How many days will I receive the edited version of the essays?

After we receive your commented version, we will email you the edited version the next day.

3. How many times do you edit the essays?

For each essay, we will edit it three times for free. After that, if you need further editing, we will charge you $20 each time.

4. How do I pay?

We accept AliPay and PayPal payments. After you request a service, we will email you the contract, which includes the payment information for final payment.

5. Which fields/majors have you helped students apply to?

We have essay writing experience in computer science, electrical engineer, data science, statistics, economy, MBA, business analytics, finance, biology, chemistry, food science, animal science, veterinary medicine, psychology, education, graphic design, new media, etc.

6. Can I see some examples of the essays you wrote?

Yes, please refer to Contact Us, and request a sample of our writings through email.

7. I want to apply for a master's program in XX major. Can you recommend some good programs?

Yes. We provide guidance on school selection in our consulting service. Please refer to Contact Us, and tell us your major and the number of schools you want to apply to. We will send you a quote.
